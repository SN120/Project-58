My Favourite Player

Pierre-Emerick Emiliano François Aubameyang is a player for Premier League club Arsenal and Gabon national team. Aubameyang has played for A.C. Milan, Saint Éttine and Borussia Dortmund. He has had a hundred appearances in the Premier League and has scored 57 goals. Aubameyang ranks as Gabon's all-time top goalscorer. Aubameyang was named African Footballer of the Year, becoming the first Gabonese player, and second-European born player, to win the award.
